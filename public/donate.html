Giving

Your generosity helps us bless children and their families by providing a transformative education

It's a partnership

Imagine the powerful impact your gift could make. By giving you are partnering with us in the opportunity to not just touch lives, but profoundly transform them. We are devoted to uplifting children and their families from poverty providing them with a truly transformative education. We know you believe, as we do, in the potential that lies within every child. Your contribution will serve as a catalyst for this potential, propelling children towards a future they could only dream of. Join us and let's make dreams come true together. Let's inspire, let's empower, let's transform lives together.

Your giving is going directly to building a school to provide 100% free education to children coming from impovershied households.

Thank you for choosing to give towards the vision and mission of Aspire Academy.

Heart Flourings

Heart Flourings is our partner organization supporting the vision and mission by providing a platform for fundraising and fiscal sponsorship. Hearts Flourings handles your giving and will deliver 100% of it to Aspire Academy in Rwanda-Africa and provide you with giving receipts.

You are giving through Flourishing hearts to Aspire Academy. Select Aspire Academy(Rwanda) on the giving drop-down list.

Donations of every size are greately appreciated and all donations are tax deductible in the US.
